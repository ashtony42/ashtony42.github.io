Gun Rights

"Gun owners have a long, rich history in our state and throughout our nation. They and their neighbors deserve common sense reforms to ensure the rights and safety of all Ohioans."

Repealing Stand Your Ground

  • SYG is a solution that is looking for a problem. Traditional self-defense laws protect gun owners in situations in which use of a firearm is necessary; SYG not only gives permission to use deadly force when it is avoidable but makes prevention and prosecution of gun violence more difficult.

  • SYG laws consistently result in growths in total numbers of firearm related deaths, including a 7% increase in suburban counties and an 8% increase in urban counties within state who have adopted them.

  • Nationally, gun crimes committed by white shooters against people of color are 250% more likely to be considered justified than those committed against white victims by POC. In SYG states, that rate jumps to 354%.

Passing red flag laws

  • While they aren't a cure-all for gun violence, evidence shows that states with red flag laws have seen drops in suicide rates.

  • Over half of all mass shooters display warning signs before committing violent crimes. Red flag laws would help create requirements for educational and psychiatric professionals to report these signs.

  • These laws would need to be paired with measures creating strong judicial oversight to ensure the protection of Ohioans' second amendment rights.

Ensure Compliance with Background Check Laws

  • Federal law requires that licensed dealers complete a background check for all gun sales to prevent those with felonies or arrest warrants from purchasing guns. As many as 27% of felonies in Ohio are never reported in the system.
