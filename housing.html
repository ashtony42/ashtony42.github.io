Housing & Community Development

"Ohio cannot function at its best if our citizens do not have a roof to sleep under at night."

Ensuring housing affordability for both renters and owners

  • Continue moratoriums on evictions and utility shutoffs to address job losses during the COVID-19 pandemic and ensure rental assistance continues to be accessible until the pandemic is over.

  • Relieve property taxes by ensuring that large land developers pay their fair share of local and state taxes.

  • Restrict tax abatements to individuals and families living in dwellings that they personally own and/or build.

Pair Gentrification with Financial Support for Residents

  • Gentrification prices homeowners and their families out of neighborhoods in which they have lived for upwards of generations. We must limit the associated ballooning of property taxes in areas under development to protect these residents.

  • Grant tax breaks to residents and business owners in low-income areas and neighborhoods populated predominantly by Ohioans of color to help ensure reinvestment directly into these communities instead of real estate companies.
